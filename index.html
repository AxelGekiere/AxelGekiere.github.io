About Us

The Gaming Industry Hub was founded by entrepreneurs in the gaming industry who were fustrated about the sheltered landscape in the industry which makes cross-company or cross-vertical collaborations harder than it should be. We saw that there was a great interest by professionals in the industry to talk with peers, however, LinkedIn or Facebook groups are not good at driving conversations, nor do they succeed in channeling different conversations to different places.

That's why we relied on the #1 instant messaging tool that gamers use: Discord. Our discord server offers a way to easily talk to relevant profiles and jump in on existing conversations about different topics of the gaming industry.
